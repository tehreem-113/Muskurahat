# 🦷 Muskaan (مسکان) — Digital Oral Health Platform

## 📌 Project Overview 🌟

**Muskaan (مسکان)** is a digital oral health platform prototype tailored for Pakistan. The project serves as a comprehensive dental health awareness application designed to bridge the gap between local communities and accessible oral healthcare. 

The platform focuses on two primary goals: raising public awareness regarding dental hygiene and providing easy-access digital tools to simplify finding and booking appointments at nearby clinics. 🩺✨

---

### 🎯 Key Objectives

* **📢 Public Health Awareness:** Offering accessible, bilingual educational resources to educate users on oral hygiene, including localized health risk awareness campaigns (such as the hazards of Chaalia and Gutka usage).
* **🏥 Simplified Clinic Access:** Empowering users to discover local dental clinics, verify their credentials, and schedule checkups seamlessly through an intuitive digital interface.
* **🪥 Proactive Oral Hygiene Habits:** Integrating interactive tools—such as a guided brushing timer, habit streaks, and toothbrush replacement trackers—to help users build and sustain healthy daily dental routines.

---

### ✨ Core Features

* **🌐 Bilingual Educational Hub (`Urdu / English`):** Seamless toggling between English and Urdu health resources to ensure widespread community accessibility. 🇵🇰
* **🔍 Smart Clinic Locator:** A searchable, filterable directory allowing users to sort nearby dental clinics by city and easily verify **PMDC (Pakistan Medical & Dental Council)** certification status.
* **📅 Streamlined 3-Step Booking:** A user-friendly appointment workflow that guides patients smoothly from clinic selection to date/time reservation and final confirmation. 
* **⏱️ Interactive Wellness Dashboard:** Built-in patient care utilities featuring a synchronized 2-minute brushing timer with quadrant prompts, digital record lists, and automated care-reminder checklists.
* **💬 Instant Guidance Assistant:** A dedicated, floating chatbot companion designed to offer responsive, immediate dental health guidance and answer common oral care queries.
